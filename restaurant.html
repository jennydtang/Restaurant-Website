Here are my Favorite Five Places to Eat in Charlotte!

I'm a little biased since I'm from Charlotte, but it is one of my favorite cities! While here, you won't have any trouble finding some great restaurants.
Below is a list of my top five, but there are many more great restaurants! Don't be afraid to go out and explore!

The Top 5 Restaurants in Charlotte

  1. Sabor Latin Street Grill
    • Location: 14 locations and counting in Charlotte and cities nearby! The one located at 1616 Camden Road is home to the South End population!
    • Sabor has great Latin Street food. It provides a casual dining experience with choices of burritos, nachos, tacos, and more!
  2. Midwood Smokehouse
    • Location: 5 locations in Charlotte and cities nearby! You can visit the one on 540-C Brandywine Rd if you want to take a quick trip from South End!
    • While in the south, have some BBQ! Midwood Smokehouse serves pulled pork, ribs, burnt ends, and more that don't disappoint! Even their salads are delicious!
  3. Doan's Vietnamese Cuisine
    • Location: The Address is: 5937 South Blvd, Charlotte, NC 28217.
    • Charlotte is home to some excellent Vietnamese cuisine. Come visit Doan's if you want killer bowl of pho or any other Vietnamese dish! They have been in Charlotte for years, and my family knows their family. I love supporting small businesses!
  4. Viva Chicken
    • Location: Viva Chicken has 14 locations and counting. I first started visiting Viva Chicken years ago at: 1617 Elizabeth Avenue.
    • Viva Chicken serves awesome Peruvian cuisine! Who knew roasted chicken could be so good! Don't skip the sauces...or the plantains and yuca fries.
  5. Jenny's Pizza
    • Location: I am Jenny, and this is my kitchen! I have 1 location plus a food truck so I keep moving! You can visit my kitchen at: 1908 East Blvd. in Charlotte
    • Have you ever met a person who doesn't like pizza? I don't think I have! The pizza at Jenny's will surely please any palate. We specialize in making pizza using flavors from around the world.
